61:         <h2><a href="/hardware">Beagle Board Details</a></h2>
62:         <p>Beagle Board is an ultra-low cost, high performance, low power OMAP3 based platform designed by BeagleBoard.org community members and sold by Digi-Key.</p>
63:         <h3>Hardware:</h3>
64:         <ul>
65:          <li><a href="http://focus.ti.com/general/docs/gencontent.tsp?contentId=36915">OMAP3530 processor</a> delivers laptop-like performance at handheld power levels</li>
66:          <li>Over 1,200 Dhrystone MIPS using the superscalar ARM Cortex-A8 with highly accurate       branch prediction and 256KB L2 cache running at up to 600MHz</li>
67:          <li>OpenGL<sup>&copy;</sup> ES 2.0 capable 2D/3D graphics accelerator capable of rendering 10 million       polygons per second</li>
68:          <li>HD video capable TMS320C64x+&trade DSP for versatile signal processing at up to 430MHz</li>
69:          <li>USB power</li>
70:         </ul>
